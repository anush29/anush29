Why Anush should go to Discovery High School

6.75 credits are standard at Discovery HS while on the other hand, you only get 6 credits each year at Camas HS at max. Discovery HS includes AP classes that are maditory for everyone in the school (making everyone learn the same subject to discuss on), and will offer more AP classes for the 2019-2020 year of Freshmen and Sophomores. Discovery HS includes Fabrication Lab, Music Editing, Robotics and more interesting electives that are useful for college years and jobs. The fact that it allows you to do personal projects will give you a better understanding of your future career path but it also gives you further connections with companies because you have to connect with certain companies for a project. Also working in a Project Based Learning environment will look good on a college resume because of the amount of projects with people you will have to do will give you good people skills. They have a really good lab for building things that are industrial in worthiness. Teachers are less demanded throughout the school because comparing the options, 2,901 kids compared to 263 students enrolled next year. You have more personal time with teachers. The science labs are fully equipped You would be better at public speaking because for your projects, you will be forced to have to explain things. The campus is equipped with a variety of technologically advanced items. It is more like a private school. The food is 10x better that other Camas schools. A smaller class size means more student attention and you will have a better chance of achieving better. The schedule is 5 minutes longer than other Camas schools. 12 students from Anush’s Homeroom class are attending Discovery HS
